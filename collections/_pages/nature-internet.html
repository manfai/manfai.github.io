scope of work

Improve the communication
between human and trees online

Collect the data and at the same time not disturbing the trees. Enhance the communication between people and trees. The function is testing the atmospheric pressure, wind velocity, temperature and humidity. Collecting environmental sounds data. Checking the amount of Oxygen and carbon dioxide. Moreover, we will add an interactive function, so that people can communicate with the trees by mobile application. There will be a sound or picture feedback when someone has left a comment. Also, touching the tree will trigger the tree to play sound.
